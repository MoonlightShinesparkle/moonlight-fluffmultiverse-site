Moonbelle [usually known as belle] is a felis vulpes [cat-fox] which has the capacity to generate fire with his paws, he was adopted by Sakura's parents and has learned to bake thanks to them.

Height: 1.5 m
Species: felis vulpes [kitten fox/cat-fox]
Luminance: pyroluminance
Random aspects: can drink as much chocolate and coffee as he wants without suffering secundary effects; he usually lights stoves with his paws
Belle and Sakura consider each other sisters, Belle being the little one by a year. Sakura jokingly insists on calling Belle her sister, which he takes well. Both like to be together and love spending time together, sometimes sharing eachother recipes they think about and other times bake along with their parents and learn a new recipe or make one of their recipes better (and also enjoying the result of hard work)
