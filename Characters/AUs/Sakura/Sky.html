The fluffs Sakura AUs Sky
Sakura Sky Wafer Blossom

Sakura [usually known as Sky] is a wolf who has the objective of being able to understand the spark and explore the multiverse.

Height: 1.75 m
Species: Canius lupus lupus
Luminance: N/A
Random aspects: Manages the facility she owns, offers pies if you leave yourself be researched if you're of interest when it comes to researching
Sakura decided to make a research facility in order to be able to create more powerful technology, one of them being battery sized fusion reactors. One day she found a way to teleport herself to other places: the amorphous crystaline energetic matter translocator (Or rift for short) which left her upgrade the facility to research teleportation and other universes. One day she tried making a rift but decided to change the recipe of it, it ended up creating a rift capable of teleporing you to different universes on their universe. After some time she found out how to make rifts enter another multiverse and gave Moonstar the modified rift so they could keep on exploring and finding stuff that may benefit them.
The facility
The facility itself can be considered multiple buildings of different kinds and purposes, all interconnected via rifts. Many of these buildings have their air filtered to avoid heavy metals as people who can't withstand them come to be researched in some cases, also to increase air quality in different operations.
The facility has multiple factory facilities, a metallurgy site and a storage site, on the research facility there's multiple experimenting rooms on the which people go through different studies to be researched, a set of offices, a set of labs, a break room and a kitchen. The facility's storage also has a docking bay for prime resources to come to be used by the facility.
Some empty rooms may be found, these are used to develop and create different tools, but in some cases they are developed in the place they're needed. The facilty manages resources via crates, these contain different parts that may be needed or that are going to be sold.
