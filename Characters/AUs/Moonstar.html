Moonstar [usually known as Star] is a felis vulpes [cat-fox] which has the capacity to freeze stuff with his paws, he helps Sakura on her objective of being able to understand the spark and explore the multiverse

Height: 1.5 m
Species: felis vulpes [kitten fox/cat-fox]
Luminance: cryoluminance
Random aspects: paints his hair purple on some of its tips and paints white dots on it
Moonstar has a rosequartz rift which leaves him teleport through the multiverse he lives at, he tries to compile information on the spark's effects, but he also enjoys seeing his AUs and sometimes helping them, he isn't usually seen when researching unless he reaches the moons himself. An intereting thing about the crystal is how it can show the AUs you have by holding it
Lore
Moonstar has traveled through the multiverse using his rift, gathering information that could be useful for misc stuff such as understanding the rift or stuff that could lead to inventions or inventions themselves that could help them or their universe.
One time he decided to teleport to another multiverse: something that wasn't done before by them. Sakura trusted Star with this mission as it would be able to lead to more research explorations, so he decided to start his mission. Star prepared himself with his tools and decided to travel to the other multiverse.
Star appeared on the same multiverse Moonlight teleported to by accident with his collar and found a lost bow: one that looked like the one Sakura made to him. He then decided to investigate the scene as it seemed he wasn't the first moon to travel to another multiverse. Moonstar decided to look at the nearby cafe and found Moonlight talking to Basten, he decided to listen nearby without them noticing and with the stuff he could hear he concluded that Moonlight got lost due to an issue with his collar, it was then that he wondered how his collar worked, if he had other stuff like that, after that he decided that he would like to find that moon in the future when he comes back home.
