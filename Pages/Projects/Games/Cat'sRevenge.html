Cat's revenge

You take the role of a cat whose items where stolen by the surrounding people, seeing how your house was quite left in ruins you decide to go around in the look of them in order to get your items back and get your revenge on them

Data

  • Windows game

  • Open source
  • Downloadable

The objective

Your objective is simple: go to the different areas in look of the items they left in the place inside of crates and other misc, be careful of the people there though, as they aren't willing to give you your items back

The controls

The controls are simple:
A and D are used to move
Y and Space are used to jump
Click in order to use your magic against enemies and objects
Right click when you have < 10 magic to recover some
Collect the items you find in your path by walking on them
Shining objects are breakable

The enemies

There's 3 enemies you will be able to find through the game:

The cat deals a moderate ammount of damage, meanwhile the bunny deals a small ammount of damage but deals it quickly, but the fox deals a huge ammount of damage, albeit requiring more time to prepare an attack

Source code

This game is source code, it was made with godot 3.4.4 with the LUA language plugin, this was my first ever project in godot and every asset in it was made by me, except for the plugin of course, you're free to modify the game to your pleasure and use the assets, just make sure to mention me if you decide to use the assets in it
