Main The fluffs The planet The spark
The spark is a special lightning bolt which strucks the planet Neutrum every month
Effects
When someone gets hit by the lightning bolt, part of the energy is kept on the person, due to it the person is capable of controlling a specific kind of energy.
The energy the person controls seems to be random; the name of the process on the which the person gets struck is called "luminance"
Luminances
While the process itself is called luminance, someone being capable of controlling a certain kind of energy is considered prefixluminance, where prefix is the kind of energy you can control.
The ammount of energy the person controls varies on the ammount of energy that stayed on the person and the practice the person has put into it, other variables such as illnesses and how much the person has eaten and slept also impact on it.
Electroluminance
Electroluminance gives people the capacity to store, generate and transfer electrons, this luminance tends to make their biology completely depend on electrons as a source of energy.
Electroluminiscent people can technically also generate heat through this, but due to them not being able to tolerate such heat they avoid this.
Electroluminance's weakness is thermal energy, avoiding them from doing some actions that pyroluminance can do.
Pyroluminance
Pyroluminance gives people the capacity to control the increment of thermal energy and capacity of starting combustion on certain objects or generate a flame. Pyroluminance tends on making people capable of resisting huge ammounts of heat.
While they can resist huge ammounts of heat, they have a limit on how much they can handle. Their weakness is cold.
Cryoluminance
Cryoluminance gives people the capacity to control the decrement of thermal energy and capacity to withstand these temperature.
Cryoluminance tends on making people unable to be frozen at a certain extent and makes them incapable of suffering hipothermia at a certain extent.
While they can resist huge lack of heat, they have a limit on how much they can freeze something. Their weakness is heat.
Photoluminance
Photoluminance gives people the capacity of controlling electromagnetic radiation by generating, redirecting or absorbing it.
Photoluminance tends on making people resistant to ionizing radiation.
While they can control photons, they usually can't generate higly energetic photons for a long time. Their weakness is electricity as they tend to be more conductive and less resistant to heat.
Kineluminance
Kineluminance gives people the capacity of controlling kinetic energy by giving it or removing it to objects, they can also change the direction of it as this force tends to be in vectors.
While they can manipulate this energy, they have a huge difficulty on manipulating it on smaller particles, making them unable to change the kinetic energy of viruses and hardly be able to move bacteria.
The ammount of energy they can manipulate varies on the size, mass and volume of the object or the quantity of energy if it is redirected or removed.
Integration with biology
The process of luminance tends of integrating the absorved energy to the person's biology, it can be partial or total and also seems completely random.
Partial
The most common integration is partial, where the body doens't depends on the energy accumulated and barely benefits from the presence of it.
Partial integration leaves the person capable of using this energy as much as they want, sometimes to the point of it running out but leaving little to no consequences, the most affecting possible consequence is a feeling of slight tiredness or hunger.
Total
Total integration leaves the person dependant of the energy, making the person capable of using a bigger ammount of this on more circumtances and giving them different properties, but leave higher consequences when the person starts running out of energy.
Someone dependant on the energy requires it to be awake and be capable of doing day to day actions, this makes their blood completely be replaced by a flow of energy with the capacity of carrying compounds, a great ability to recover from massive damage but with the downside of passing out after recieving such ammounds of damage or losing a lot of energy, being capable of stopping being passed out by recharging this energy, but this is mostly limited to pyroluminance, electroluminance and Photoluminance.
This happens as the cells of the person depend on the energy as their source of energy. Total integration leaves the person turn food directly into energy, it also makes an environment where microorganisms can't trive.
Electroluminiscent and Pyroluminiscent people are more probable of having total integration, this integration type leaves them use more energy than partial integration but have to be more careful about how they use the energy.
Totally dependent people can condense their body into a ball of electricity when there's huge damage on the person as a defense mechanism, they cannot control themselves in this form and are mostly inert, they tend to stay in this shape for hours until they're recovered from it.
